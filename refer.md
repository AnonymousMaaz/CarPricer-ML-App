### **What are We Doing**
> We have a dataset that contains information about used cars. It states out some of the characteristics of the car and the parameters on which once can state the price of the car.
>
---

### **Details of the Project**
>
> The project is divided into three parts:
>
> 1. Building a machine learning model **(RandomForestRegressor)** to predict the selling price of the car.
> 2. We build a web app on Django to mount the prediction model.
> 3. We deploy the Django Project on **Heroku** through github.
